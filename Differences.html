The differences between a wired and wireless network

Wireless Networks are considered to be the future, no need for and annoying clutter of wires, just a simple Bluetooth or Wi-fi connection.

To start off what are the similarities:

Well, obviously they both are able to connect to different types of networks with ease and after set up correctly can be used for all sorts of things.

However, there are many differences. One being the layout and difficulty of both. With a wireless network you don't have to worry about where certain wires will go as there are none, however, with this the connection may be a little bit delayed as you are not directly connecting to a network but using an internet service like Wi-fi.
