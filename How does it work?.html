How do Wireless Networks actually work?

Basically, Wireless Networks broadcast a sort of signal which other devices can detect. They then tune into it and can be given access to the things on a wired network like an internet connection.

When it comes to Bluetooth, they are mainly used to tranfer data over short distances however the same method applies, and they use wavelength radio waves in order to connect the two devices.
